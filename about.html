About Great Properties GA

Our Story

Built on Trust & Results

Great Properties GA, LLC is Georgia's premier real estate investment and redevelopment company, dedicated to providing fast, fair solutions for homeowners across the state.

We specialize in buying properties in any condition — from move-in ready homes to distressed properties that need significant work. Our mission is simple: provide homeowners with a stress-free alternative to the traditional real estate process, while creating value in the communities we serve.

Unlike traditional real estate agents who simply list your property, we are direct buyers. That means no waiting months for a buyer, no costly repairs, no agent commissions, and no uncertain closing dates. When we make you an offer, we mean it — and we close on your timeline.

Our Mission

To provide homeowners with transparent, fast, and fair real estate solutions while revitalizing Georgia communities through thoughtful property renovation and reinvestment.

No middlemen. We use our own capital to purchase properties directly, which means faster closings and fewer complications.
