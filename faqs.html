Common Questions About Selling to Us

Will you buy my house in any condition?
Yes — absolutely. We buy properties in any condition, anywhere in Georgia. It doesn't matter if it needs major repairs, has fire damage, has tenants, or hasn't been maintained in years. We make cash offers on all of it.
How quickly can you close?
We can close in as little as 7 days. If you need more time to move or wrap up your affairs, we work on your schedule. We are flexible — you tell us the timeline that works for you.
Are there any fees or commissions?
None. Zero. We pay all standard closing costs and there are no agent commissions. The offer we make is what you walk away with. No surprises at the closing table.
Do I need to make repairs before you buy?
No repairs are necessary. We buy houses as-is. You don't need to clean, paint, fix, or stage anything. Just take what you want and leave the rest — we handle it from there.
How do you determine your offer price?
We evaluate the property's current condition, location, recent comparable sales in the area, and the cost of any renovations needed. We aim to make a fair offer that works for both sides — we're not here to lowball anyone.
What types of properties do you buy?
We buy single-family homes, condos, townhomes, multi-family units (duplexes, triplexes, apartment buildings), commercial properties, vacant land, and lots — anywhere in Georgia.
What areas of Georgia do you buy in?
All of Georgia. Atlanta metro, Savannah, Augusta, Columbus, Macon, Albany, Rome, Valdosta, Warner Robins — everywhere. If you own property in Georgia, we'll consider it.
My house is in foreclosure. Can you still help?
Yes — in many cases we can help stop or delay a foreclosure by buying your property quickly, often before the bank takes over. Call us immediately at 404-590-1613 to discuss your situation.
I inherited a property I don't want. Can you buy it?
Absolutely. Inherited properties are one of the most common situations we help with. We understand the complexity of probate and estate situations and can guide you through the process with minimal stress.
Is there any obligation when I submit my info?
None whatsoever. Getting an offer from us is 100% free and there is zero obligation to accept. We present you with a number, you decide — simple as that.
How is this different from listing with a Realtor?
Listing with an agent means open houses, showings, months on the market, repair requests, financing contingencies, and 5–6% in commissions. We eliminate all of that — cash, fast, as-is, no fees.
