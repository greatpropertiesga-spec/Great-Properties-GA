Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling your home to us.

How does your home buying process work?
Our process is simple: (1) You contact us and tell us about your property. (2) We research it and make you a fair cash offer within 24 hours. (3) If you accept, we handle all the paperwork and close on your timeline — as fast as 7 days.
Do I need to make repairs before selling?
Absolutely not. We buy homes in any condition — whether it needs minor cosmetic updates or major structural repairs. You don't spend a single dollar fixing anything. We handle all of that after purchase.
How do you determine your cash offer?
We look at the location, size, condition, and recent sales of comparable homes in your area. Our goal is to make a fair offer that works for both sides — we're not here to lowball anyone.
Are there any fees or commissions?
None whatsoever. There are no real estate agent commissions, no closing costs, and no hidden fees. The cash offer we make is the full amount you receive at closing.
How fast can you close?
We can close in as little as 7 days if needed. If you need more time to make arrangements, we can also wait — the closing date is always on your schedule, not ours.
What types of properties do you buy?
We buy single-family homes, condos, townhomes, multi-family properties, commercial buildings, land, and lots — anywhere in Georgia, in any condition.
What situations do you help with?
We help homeowners in many situations: foreclosure or pre-foreclosure, divorce, inherited properties, job loss or relocation, tired landlords, fire or water damage, code violations, tax liens, and more. No situation is too complicated for us.
Is your cash offer an obligation?
No, our cash offers are 100% free and come with zero obligation. If our offer doesn't work for you, simply decline — no pressure, no hard feelings. We want the deal to be right for you.
Do you cover the entire state of Georgia?
Yes! We buy properties across all of Georgia including Atlanta, Savannah, Augusta, Macon, Columbus, Athens, Albany, Valdosta, Gainesville, and every city and county in between.
