Frequently Asked Questions

Do you buy homes in any condition?+
Yes — absolutely any condition. Fire damage, structural issues, code violations, hoarder situations, liens, tenant problems — we've seen it all. We make offers on every type of property regardless of condition.
How fast can you close?+
We can typically close in 7 to 14 days. If you need more time, we can close on your schedule — we've worked around 60+ day timelines too.
Is there any obligation when I request an offer?+
Zero obligation. Getting an offer from us is completely free and there is no pressure whatsoever. You can decline at any time.
Will I need to make repairs before selling?+
No. We buy properties as-is. Whatever condition your property is in today is exactly the condition we'll buy it in.
What areas of Georgia do you buy in?+
All of Georgia — Atlanta metro, Savannah, Augusta, Columbus, Macon, Albany, Athens, Warner Robins, and every city and county in between.
How do you determine your offer price?+
We consider the property's current condition, comparable sales in the area, estimated repair costs, and current market conditions. We aim to make a fair offer that works for both sides.
Do I need to clean out the property?+
Leave whatever you don't want. We handle the cleanout after closing.
Are there any fees or commissions?+
None. We cover all closing costs. What we offer is what you receive.
Can you buy my home if it's in foreclosure?+
Yes. If you're facing foreclosure, contact us immediately — we may be able to purchase your home before the foreclosure completes and help you avoid the credit damage.
What types of properties do you buy?+
Single-family homes, condos, townhomes, multi-family units (duplexes, triplexes, apartment buildings), commercial properties, land, and lots.
How is this different from listing with an agent?+
When you list with an agent, you wait months, pay 5-6% in commissions, make repairs, host showings, and risk deals falling through. With us, you get a cash offer in 24 hours and close in days — with zero fees.
